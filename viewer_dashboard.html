About the Project

The Ethiopian Integrated Infrastructure Spatial Data Visualization Portal is a national digital platform designed to unify and visualize spatial data from key federal infrastructure organizations and government stakeholders. It provides a centralized, interactive environment where decision-makers, planners, and technical experts can monitor, analyze, and coordinate infrastructure assets and development projects across sectors. At the same time, it leverages modern GIS and data visualization technologies to enhance situational awareness, reduce duplication of investments, and support evidence-based decision-making. This makes infrastructure planning more efficient, transparent, and future-oriented, aligning with Ethiopiaâ€™s broader digital transformation and sustainable development goals. By integrating data from Ethiopian Electric Power, Ethio Telecom, Safaricom Ethiopia, Ethiopian Electric Utility, Ethiopian Airlines, Ethiopian Railway Corporation, Ethiopian Roads Authority, Ethiopian Industry Park Development Corporation, and the Ministry of Urban and Infrastructure, the portal strengthens inter-agency collaboration, policy alignment, and strategic planning.
